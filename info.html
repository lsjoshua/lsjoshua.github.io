JOSHUA LEE


☰

Photographer and video-maker from England, currently based in Tokyo

Photographing on film since 2018 with an affinity for landscapes, loud music, and portraits. Nowadays I use a healthy mixture of film and digital photography, with a love for natural, moody, documentary-style images. Nowadays as a resident of Tokyo for over 3 years, I've spent much of my time here documenting the city's underground music scene, from extreme heavy metal to shoegaze, alternative pop, and noise.

Formerly a writer on music, I've also dabbled in audio production, recorded and released my own music, and put together this website in code.

Send me an email at contact@joshlee.photo, or reach out to me on my socials linked above if you'd like to get in touch.
