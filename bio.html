MUSIC・PHOTO・CONTACT




I'm a musician, photographer and writer based in Tokyo.

My photography concerns landscapes, live music and portraiture. Recently I've also been dabbling in video.

I write guitar-led ambient music as PALE STAR, influenced by bands like Sunn O))), Godspeed You! Black Emperor and Earth, as well as nature and cinema.

Formerly an in-house writer on jazz music, as well as for various alternative music press.
